When is the best time to visit Cambodia?

The cool dry season (November-February) is most popular, but each season offers unique advantages. November brings beautiful water lilies at Angkor, while the green season (May-October) offers lush landscapes and fewer crowds.

Do I need a visa for Cambodia?

Most nationalities can obtain a visa on arrival (tourist visa $30) or apply for an e-visa online. We recommend checking requirements based on your passport country.

Are your tours suitable for families?

Absolutely! Cambodia is very family-friendly. Many of our tours can be customized for children, and we can recommend age-appropriate activities.
